#+TITLE: ox-ghost Version History

* 0.8.0 (2026-01-31)

- Added =ghost-pull-metadata= to refresh org headers from Ghost
- Added =ghost-status= to show sync diff
- =ghost-update= now auto-detects post from =#+GHOST_ID:= header
